Data Safety

This page explains how SurpriseUp collects, uses, stores, and protects user information. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Required
Name Display your profile and personalize your experience. Yes (Google Sign-In)
Email Address User authentication and account identification. Yes (Google Sign-In)
Advertising ID Rewarded advertisements and ad performance. Collected by Google AdMob
Device Information Security, analytics, crash reporting, and ad delivery. Collected by Google services

How Your Information Is Used

  • Sign in securely using your Google account.
  • Display your profile information.
  • Create and manage your SurpriseUp account.
  • Award credit points after rewarded advertisements.
  • Create Surprise Links and access premium features.
  • Improve application performance and reliability.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse.

Information Stored on Your Device

To improve performance and reduce unnecessary network requests, SurpriseUp stores the following information locally:

  • User Name
  • Email Address

This information is stored only for convenience and is not shared from your device with third parties except as required for the app's functionality.

Third-Party Services

SurpriseUp uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Google AdMob Rewarded Ads
  • Google Play Services

These providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Your information is shared only when necessary to provide core app functionality, such as:

  • User authentication through Google.
  • Rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Compliance with legal obligations when required.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
