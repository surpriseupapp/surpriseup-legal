When you watch rewarded advertisements, Google and its advertising partners may collect information including:

Your information is used to:

  • Allow creation of surprise links and access to premium features using earned credits.

  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

Users can earn credit points by watching rewarded advertisements.

Credit points have no monetary value and cannot be redeemed for cash unless explicitly stated by SurpriseUp.
