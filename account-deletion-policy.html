Account Deletion Policy

At SurpriseUp, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your account and what happens to your information after your request is processed.

How to Request Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your SurpriseUp account at any time by contacting us at:

Email: your-email@example.com

To help us verify your identity and process your request efficiently, please include:

  • Your registered Google email address.
  • The subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once your request is verified, we will permanently delete your account and associated personal information, including:

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your SurpriseUp account information.
  • Your earned credit points.
  • Your app preferences associated with your account.
  • Your Surprise Links and other user-generated content stored on our servers, unless retention is required by law.

What Data May Be Retained?

Certain information may be retained for a limited period when required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Resolve disputes.
  • Prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Enforce our Terms & Conditions.

Anonymous analytics, crash reports, and aggregated usage statistics collected through Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics may also be retained because they cannot reasonably be linked back to an individual user.

Deletion Timeline

We aim to process verified account deletion requests within 7 business days. In some cases, additional time may be required due to technical or legal reasons.

Effect of Account Deletion

  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion.
  • Your earned credit points will be permanently removed.
  • Your Surprise Links may no longer be accessible.
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use SurpriseUp again.

Third-Party Services

Deleting your SurpriseUp account does not automatically delete information held by third-party service providers such as:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

These services manage their own data according to their respective privacy policies.
